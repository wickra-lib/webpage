# Privacy Policy

This site does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any third-party
tracking, and it collects no personal data of its own. The notes below explain
the little that does happen technically, and your rights.

## Who is responsible

The controller for this website is **Wickra**, Switzerland. For any
privacy-related request, contact **[support@wickra.org](mailto:support@wickra.org)**.

## No cookies, no tracking

`wickra.org` sets **no cookies** and runs **no analytics, advertising, or
tracking scripts**. Your visit is not profiled, and no behavioural data is
collected or shared.

## Hosting and server logs

The site is a static site hosted on **Cloudflare Pages** (Cloudflare, Inc.).
Like any web host, Cloudflare automatically processes technical connection data
— your IP address, browser user-agent, the requested URL, and a timestamp — in
server logs in order to deliver the pages and protect the site against attacks.
This is carried out by Cloudflare as our hosting and CDN provider.

- **Legal basis:** legitimate interest in operating a secure, performant website
  (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR, where applicable) and the corresponding provisions of the
  Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP).
- **International transfer:** Cloudflare is a US-based company, so this technical
  data may be processed outside Switzerland and the EU under appropriate
  safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses / the EU–US Data Privacy Framework).
  See [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Browser storage

The site stores a small amount of data in your browser's **local storage** for
functional purposes only — remembering your light/dark theme preference and the
on-page search index. This data is first-party, stays on your device, is never
transmitted to us, and is not used for tracking. You can clear it at any time
through your browser settings.

## The interactive demo

The [live demo](/demo) runs entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. It makes
no network requests and sends no data anywhere.

## External links

Some pages link to third-party sites (GitHub, crates.io, PyPI, npm,
docs.wickra.org). When you follow such a link, the destination's own privacy
policy applies; we have no control over how those sites process your data.

## Your rights

Under the Swiss FADP and the GDPR (where it applies) you have the right to
access, rectification, erasure, restriction of, and objection to the processing
of your personal data, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory
authority. As we store no personal data ourselves beyond the host's technical
logs described above, please direct any such request to
**[support@wickra.org](mailto:support@wickra.org)** and we will assist.

We never sell data, and we perform no profiling or automated decision-making.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves. The date below reflects the
current version.
